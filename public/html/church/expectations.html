What to Expect

Service Flow

Below is an overview of what to expect during a typical worship service.

Welcome & Greeting

Upon arrival, please enter through the main entrance and turn right, where our friendly greeters will welcome you.

Worship Through Music

We worship together through timeless hymns and songs of praise that lift our hearts to Jesus and prepare us to receive His Word.

Community Prayer

We take time to pray together as a community, lifting up needs, giving thanks, and seeking God’s care.

Teaching & Message

Each service includes a message from Scripture that connects God’s timeless truths to the real challenges and joys we face each day.

Offering & Communion

We joyfully give as an act of worship and celebrate the Lord’s Supper on the second and last Sundays of each month. All are welcome to worship; Communion is reserved for WELS members. Please speak with a pastor if you’d like to learn more.

Fellowship & Connection

After service, we encourage everyone to say hello, enjoy refreshments, and build friendships with others.

Good to Know

Practical information to help you feel prepared and comfortable

What to Wear

Come as you are! Our congregation dresses casually — jeans, khakis, dresses, or business casual are all perfectly appropriate.

Offering

Giving is never expected from visitors or members. A QR code is available in the bulletin for members who wish to give electronically.

Parking

Parking is available in our main lot and on the street nearby in the back.

Accessibility

Our building is fully wheelchair accessible with designated seating areas and hearing assistance devices available.

Children

We welcome every child to worship with us, embracing each one as part of God’s family. A mothers room is located in the back for those who need a break.

Stay Connected

Check out the bulletin for weekly announcements. Pastors will also cover any important information during the worship service.

Coffee & Refreshments

Complimentary refreshments are available before and after the service in our lobby area.

Questions

Our pastors are happy to answer questions, pray with you, or help you get connected with others.

Hearing Assistance

Hearing assistance is available upon request to help ensure
everyone can fully participate in worship.
