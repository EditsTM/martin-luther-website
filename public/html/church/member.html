How It Works

Our membership process is designed to help you grow in your understanding of the Lutheran faith and become fully integrated into our church family.

1

12-Week Course

Our comprehensive course introduces you to Lutheran theology, the teachings of Martin Luther, and the life of our congregation.

  • Weekly classes covering essential doctrine and practices
  • Time for questions and meaningful discussions
  • Build relationships with other members
2

Church Confirmation

After completing the course, you'll be confirmed and officially welcomed as a member during a special ceremony.

  • Public affirmation of your faith commitment
  • Blessing and prayer from the congregation
