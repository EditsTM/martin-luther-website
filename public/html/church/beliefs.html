Our Beliefs

What We Believe

  • God

    One God in three persons—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—eternal, almighty, and merciful.

  • The Bible

    God’s inspired and inerrant Word, our final authority for faith and life.

  • Creation

    God created all things by His Word and sustains His creation with care.

  • Man & Woman

    Humanity is created in God’s image with dignity, designed for relationship with Him.

  • Sin

    All have sinned and fall short of God’s glory, separated from Him without grace.

  • The Gospel

    The good news that Jesus lived, died, and rose to save sinners—God’s gift to us.

  • Jesus

    True God and true man, the promised Savior who redeems us by His cross and empty tomb.

  • Salvation

    By grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone—not by works—so no one may boast.

  • Faith

    The Holy Spirit creates and strengthens faith through the Word and Sacraments.

  • The Church

    All believers in Christ, gathered by His Word, sent to proclaim His grace.

  • Baptism & Holy Communion

    Gospel gifts that deliver forgiveness and strengthen faith by God’s promise.

  • Peace

    In Christ we have peace with God now and the hope of eternal life.
