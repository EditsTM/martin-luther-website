Early Childhood Program

Martin Luther Early Child Ministries

Martin Luther’s Early Childhood ministry assists families by nurturing the development and spiritual foundation of young children in a Christ-centered environment.

The Martin Luther Early Childhood program is offering new, expanded program options for 3 and 4-year-olds!

Children age 3 or 4 by September 1 are eligible.
