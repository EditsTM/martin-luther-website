About Our School

Building Faith, Character & Knowledge

Martin Luther Evangelical Lutheran Church & School has been a cornerstone of Christ-centered education in our community. As a proud member of the Wisconsin Evangelical Lutheran Synod (WELS), we are committed to nurturing the whole child through excellent academics grounded in Biblical truth.

Our dedicated faculty creates a supportive environment where students grow spiritually, academically, and socially. We believe that every child is a gift from God, uniquely created with special talents and purposes.

Our Mission

Martin Luther School partners with families to provide a Christ-centered education that equips students with knowledge, skills, and values firmly rooted in God's Word. We prepare young hearts and minds to serve Christ in their daily lives and share the Gospel message with a world in need of a Savior.

WELS Affiliation

As a member of the Wisconsin Evangelical Lutheran Synod, we stand firmly on the inspired, inerrant Word of God. Our commitment to confessional Lutheran doctrine ensures that Biblical truth permeates every aspect of our educational ministry.
